Search for UTF-8 files with BOM

via https://stackoverflow.com/a/2858757/3449199

Just find out UTF-8 files with BOM:

  grep -rl $'\xEF\xBB\xBF' .

Find out UTF-8 files with BOM and then remove BOM:

  find . -type f -exec sed '1s/^\xEF\xBB\xBF//' -i {} \;
